1. PROTECT - Help protect children from abuse and violence. Support prevention and healing programs that help families survive and recover during times of crisis.

2. Respond - Long-term programs, beyond emergency relief and in partnership with the World Food Program, to bring lasting change in crisis zones.

3. Equip - Help families thrive with school supplies, clean water, health kits, and training, giving communities tools for a stronger, sustainable future.

4. Empower - Uplift women, girls, and communities by supporting long-term solutions like business training, savings groups, education, and leadership programs.
